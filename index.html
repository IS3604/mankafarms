About Manka Farms

Your Enchanting

Farmhouse Escape

Welcome to Manka Farms — an enchanting farmhouse nestled amidst the lush embrace of the jungle, crafted for the discerning nature lover seeking respite from the bustling world. With four spacious and luxurious rooms, our sanctuary offers a dreamlike escape into the heart of nature.

Located in Amajhiri on the Nagpur–Jabalpur Highway, just minutes from Pench Tiger Reserve, Manka Farms is the perfect base for wildlife safaris, bird watching, and immersive jungle experiences. Sleep under the stars, gather around the fire, and wake up to the sounds of the wild.
